Meet Bryce Roth Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience and Goal

Purpose: This project will be a professional website showcasing my web development skills.

Audience: The design of the site is directed to the professional IT community within any
size of business. The target audiences are recruiters, HR managers and other IT professionals
seeking web developers.

Goal: The goal is to showcase different projects, collaborations on projects, previous
relevant work history and other soft skills. All the showcased items should be geared towards
the IT professional who is looking to add someone to their team of developers or help with
a specific project. The end goal is to gain employment using the skills learned in Deep Dive.

Persona

Name: Harvey Rutherford

Age: 43

Profession or Role: Hiring manager looking for mid-level web developers.

Technology: Works remotely at times and has a company provided laptop with a docking station,
wireless keyboard/mouse and a second monitor. Company provided equipment is a couple years old.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

Frustrations and Needs: Frustrated that he is hiring web developers that will have newer, more
up-to-date equipment than him. Needs access to potential web developers and be able to quickly see that they
have the necessary and basic skills to be a mid-level web developer.
