Meet Bryce Roth Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience and Goal

Purpose: This project will be a professional website showcasing my web development skills.

Audience: The design of the site is directed towards the professional IT community within any
size of business. The target audience is recruiters, HR managers and other IT professionals
seeking web developers.

Goal: The goal is to showcase different projects, collaborations on projects, previous
relevant work history and other soft skills. All the showcased items should be geared towards
the IT professional who is looking to add someone to their team of developers or help with
a specific project. The end goal is to gain employment using the skills learned in Deep Dive.
