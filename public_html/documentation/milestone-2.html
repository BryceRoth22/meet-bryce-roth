Content Strategy

The site about me will be a single page with four sections: Welcome section, About Me section, Projects section and a Contact Me section.

Welcome section: This will be an image of me on the right with a introductory message on the left. The introductory message will have my name and what I do (front end web developer).

About Me section: This section will list different skills I have relating to web development and also list managerial skills relating to the web development field I have gain from previous work/employment. It will list the programming languages I know, programs I use such as GitHub, etc. and other development aspects I know. This section should provide a recruiter or hiring manager the information needed to know if I am a good fit for their company or project.

Projects section: In this section there will be up to six projects. The projects will be shown as images that will have a pop up when the mouse scrolls over the image that will list my role on the project and the contribution I had in it.
